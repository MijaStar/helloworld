Juice WRLD

Image sourced from Fan Art

Jarad Higgins, known professionally as Juice WRLD, was an American rapper, singer, and songwriter. Born in Chicago, Illinois, Juice WRLD gained significant recognition through his singles "Lucid Dreams," "All Girls Are the Same," and "Legends" before his untimely passing in December 2019.

Juice WRLD gained attention on SoundCloud for his diverse musical style blending elements of emo, rock, and hip-hop, captivating listeners with his emotional lyrics and melodic approach.

Despite his short-lived career, Juice WRLD left a lasting impact on the music industry and his devoted fanbase. His posthumous albums, including "Legends Never Die" and "Goodbye & Good Riddance," continue to resonate with audiences worldwide.
