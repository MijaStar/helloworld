Chico Lachowski

"Chico" Lachowski is a Brazilian model known for his work with various fashion brands and magazines. He's recognized for his striking features and has had a successful modeling career...

Chico has worked extensively with Dior, Dolce & Gabbana, and Armani. He's been featured in publications like Vogue and GQ, showcasing his versatile modeling talent.

Jordan Barrett

Jordan Barrett is approximately 6 feet 2 inches tall (188 cm) and his weight is around 165 lbs (75 kg). He hails from Byron Bay, Australia. He's worked with several big names in the fashion industry, including designers like Tom Ford and photographers such as Mario Testino and Steven Klein.

Jordan is known for his captivating presence on the runway. His collaboration with top-tier designers and photographers has established him as a prominent figure in the fashion world.
